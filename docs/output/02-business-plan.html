1. The Productized Model

Inspired by DesignJoy ($5,995/mo, 1 designer, $1.5M+/yr revenue) and the “Productize” framework by Eisha Armstrong. We convert consulting services into products with fixed pricing, standardized delivery, and monthly subscriptions.

The 4 products

Product Type US Price LATAM (-40%) AI Startup (-25%)
Performance Managed 24/7 + board $5,995/mo $3,595/mo $4,495/mo
Observability Managed + board $6,995/mo $4,195/mo $5,245/mo
Fractional SRE Expert subscription $7,995/mo $4,795/mo $5,995/mo
Fractional QA TL Expert subscription From $6,995/mo $4,195/mo $5,245/mo
Checkup (entry) One-shot $2,995 $1,795 $2,245

Why it works

  • Eliminates sales friction: pricing on website, subscribe via Stripe, no proposals
  • Reduces CAC: no 4-8 week sales cycle. Subscribe → onboarding in 1 hour
  • Increases LTV: pause/cancel is less scary than “breaking a contract”
  • Scalable: k6 framework + automated Grafana Cloud allows more clients per engineer
  • Predictable: fixed monthly revenue per client, low fixed costs

Discount programs

  • LATAM & Spain: 40% off all plans for companies in Latin America and Spain
  • AI Startups: 25% off for early-stage teams building with AI
