FAQs

What test frameworks do you work with?

We're framework-agnostic: Jest, Vitest, Playwright, Cypress, Pytest, RSpec. We use whatever fits your stack. If you have nothing, we'll recommend based on your language and team size.

Do you write tests or teach our team?

Both, in phases. We write the first layer of coverage to establish patterns and velocity — then teach your engineers the same approach so they can maintain it independently.

How long until our team is autonomous?

Typically 60–90 days to meaningful independence. We don't optimize for dependency — a team that doesn't need us after 90 days is a success story we want to tell.

What's your coverage target?

We don't chase a coverage percentage — we chase meaningful coverage of critical paths. A 40% coverage number that covers your checkout flow is more valuable than 90% coverage of utility functions.
