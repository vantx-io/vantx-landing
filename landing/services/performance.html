Performance Questions

What performance tools do you use?

We use profiling tools native to your stack — flame graphs, APM traces, browser DevTools, load testing with k6 or Gatling. We optimize with your existing toolchain, not by adding new vendors.

How do you measure impact?

Before-and-after benchmarks on the metrics that matter: p50/p95/p99 latency, throughput, error rates, Core Web Vitals. Every optimization ships with measured proof.

Will optimizations break existing functionality?

We don't ship changes without validation. Every optimization goes through your existing CI pipeline and is tested under realistic load before production. Zero surprises.

What results are realistic in the first month?

A full performance baseline, your top 3 bottlenecks identified and fixed, and a roadmap for the next 90 days. Most teams see 30–60% improvement on their worst endpoints in week two.

Do you need access to our production environment?

For load testing, we only need a target URL and agreed-upon test parameters. For synthetic monitoring, we hit your public endpoints externally. No production access, no SSH keys, no credentials.

Will load tests affect our production traffic?

We always test against staging or a dedicated environment first. For production load tests, we coordinate timing with your team and ramp traffic gradually. You approve every test before it runs.

What tools do you use?

k6 for load testing, Grafana for dashboards, Playwright for synthetic monitoring, Lighthouse CI for web performance. All open-source. If you cancel, every script, dashboard, and config stays with you.

Can I start with the one-time Performance Checkup instead?

Yes. The Checkup is a standalone 5-day engagement: capacity baseline, load test, performance audit, and an action plan. Many clients start here and upgrade to the subscription after seeing the first report.
